WHAT IS THE

LINCOLN PAC?

The Lincoln PAC believes in honest and open government – by the people, for the people. Our goal is to support candidates who embody the same beliefs. Candidates supported by the Lincoln PAC have demonstrated their capabilities and dedication to a transparent government.

If you are a candidate seeking endorsement, please contact us at info@lincolnpac.com.
